Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before joining DevConf2026.

Who should attend DevConf2026?

Developers, students, software engineers, UI/UX designers, startup founders, and technology enthusiasts looking to learn, network, and grow.

What is included in my ticket?

Your ticket includes keynote sessions, technical workshops, networking opportunities, refreshments, conference materials, and a digital participation certificate.

Will the sessions be recorded?

Yes. Every registered attendee will receive access to session recordings after the event.

Can I transfer my ticket?

Absolutely. Ticket transfers are available up to seven days before the conference.

Are student discounts available?

Yes. Students receive discounted pricing after verifying their student identity during registration.
